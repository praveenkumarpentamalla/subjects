## 1. FOUNDATION TO ADVANCED COVERAGE

### **BASIC CONCEPTS (Foundation Level)**

#### **A. What is BODMAS?**

**Definition:** BODMAS is the rule that tells us the ORDER in which to perform mathematical operations in an expression.

**BODMAS Stands For:**
```
B - Brackets
O - Of (or Orders/Exponents)
D - Division
M - Multiplication
A - Addition
S - Subtraction
```

**Other Names (Same Rule):**
- **PEMDAS** (USA): Parentheses, Exponents, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction
- **BIDMAS** (UK): Brackets, Indices, Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction
- **GEMDAS**: Grouping, Exponents, Multiplication, Division, Addition, Subtraction

**All mean the same thing!**

---

#### **B. Why BODMAS Matters**

**Example:** 2 + 3 √ó 4

**Without BODMAS (left to right):**
```
= 5 √ó 4 = 20 ‚ùå WRONG!
```

**With BODMAS (multiply first):**
```
= 2 + 12 = 14 ‚úì CORRECT!
```

**Real-world:** You buy 2 pens at ‚Çπ5 each and 3 books at ‚Çπ10 each
- Cost = 2√ó5 + 3√ó10 = 10 + 30 = ‚Çπ40
- NOT (2+3)√ó(5+10) = ‚Çπ75!

#### **B. Special Patterns**

**1. Distributive Property:**
```

a(b - c) = ab - ac
```

**Example:** 5(3 + 7) = 5√ó10 = 50

**2. Associative Property:**
```

**3. Commutative Property:**
```

**Note:** Division and subtraction are NOT commutative!
